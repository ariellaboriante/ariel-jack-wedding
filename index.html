Our Story

How We Met

We met each other in physics class during the first year of university. Right away, we bonded over busy schedules, formula sheets, and working late nights in the engineering lab for our robotics course. However, it wasn't until the summer of 2014 that we started talking to each other and eventually we decided to meet up. We agreed to invite our friends to watch Guardians of the Galaxy at Fairview mall. To the other's surprise, we both showed up alone and said our friends couldn't make it. That was our first date. Since then we've been like peas and carrots, graduating together, travelling to new and exciting places, and renovating our first home from top to bottom. However, the greatest achievement we have yet to accomplish is to start our own family. It's been our dream for quite a while and we can't wait for you to be there with us!

How He Proposed

On April 16, 2022, Jack proposed to me in the most unique way. It happened on our spontaneous Vegas road trip where we planned to hike Angel's Landing at Zion National Park. After a chaotic start of the day which involved picking up our jeep camper, learning how to set up the attached tent, buying groceries, crossing the Nevada-Utah border and finding out we "lost" an hour because of the time change, the day transition to one where everything fell right into place. Since we arrived late, everyone was already heading down the trail so at the top of the mountain, we had the whole place to ourselves! We were at a dangerous yet scenic point in the trail and I stopped to take a video of the amazing view. When I turned around, Jack was already on one knee! He opened his wallet and produced a ring that he managed to keep from the person that packs his suitcase! Jack didn't know it but incredibly, he proposed to me in my namesake place because my name Ariel is used synonymously with "Zion". A custom proposal from a person specifically made for me!

Getting There

The easiest way to get to Banff is to fly to Calgary and then rent a car at the airport. From there, Banff is about 1 hour and 45 minutes. We recommend staying at Canmore for budget-friendly accommodations. Canmore is only a 30-minute drive away from Banff.

What is the dress code for the wedding?
Please dress in formal attire. Our ceremony will be outdoors so remember to bring a coat!

What will the weather be like in Banff this time of year?
Spring weather can be unpredictable in Banff. We recommend wearing layers and bringing a jacket. The mountain tops can be quite cold!

Where can I park?
Parking is available at the Banff gondola for free.

What happens after the ceremony?
Please stay after the ceremony for group photos. You'll have some time to explore the trail before cocktail hours starts. At 5 PM, please head to our wedding reception on the third floor for the Sky Bistro restaurant.

Does the wedding have a theme?
We ask everyone to wear blue, purple or pink in keeping with our theme!

What time should I arrive?
Please arrive before 1:30 PM to allow for the upload time on the cable car.

Are kids welcome?
